Harvard University

Harvard University

The prestigious Harvard University in Cambridge boasts stunning architecture and rich history. We walked through the Ivy League campus and taking in the peaceful surroundings.

Isabella Stewart Gardner Museum

Isabella Stewart Gardner Museum

This museum is a hidden gem with a rich collection of fine art. The museum itself is a beautiful building with lush gardens that make it a perfect day trip in Boston.

Newbury Street

Newbury Street is Boston’s premier shopping district. We wandered through the chic boutiques and cafés, soaking in the vibrant atmosphere of the city’s fashion center.

Prudential Tower

Prudential Tower

One of Boston’s tallest buildings, Prudential Tower offers spectacular views of the city. We enjoyed a scenic trip to the observation deck to take in the panoramic skyline.

Institute of Contemporary Art (ICA)

Rockport

A charming coastal town outside Boston. The streets are lined with colorful buildings, and the harbor views are postcard-perfect. We spent the day exploring the quaint shops and eating fresh seafood.

North End

The North End

The North End is Boston’s Little Italy, known for its Italian restaurants and bakeries. We had some tiramisu and tea!

Boston Common

Boston Common

Boston Common was the perfect place to relax and enjoy nature in the heart of the city. We were having a picnic and the pigeons decided to join us.
